  PC PART CHOOSER



Cases

The case is important as it holds everything in place. When purchasing a case, be sure all of your parts will fit. Check the dimensions of your parts and case to make sure they are compatible. A lot of cases have RGB and fans so make sure you want those before buying. Fans can be easily removed and replaced.
